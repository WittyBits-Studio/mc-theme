How do I join the server?
Open Minecraft Java Edition, go to Multiplayer, click Add Server and enter the server IP from the Servers page. Make sure you are using a supported version (1.20+).
Is the server free to play?
Yes, the server is completely free. You can purchase optional VIP ranks in the shop to unlock cosmetics and extra features, but all gameplay content is available without paying.
How do I buy a rank?
Visit our shop via the DOWNLOAD link in the main menu. Select your desired rank, enter your Minecraft nickname and complete the payment. The rank is applied automatically within a few minutes.
I got banned – what do I do?
If you believe the ban was a mistake, submit a ban appeal on our Discord server. Go to the #ban-appeal channel, fill in the form and a staff member will review your case within 48 hours.
How do I report a player or bug?
Use the /report command in-game to report a player, or contact a staff member on Discord. Bugs can be reported in the #bugs channel. Please include screenshots or evidence when possible.
Are mods and clients allowed?
Performance mods (OptiFine, Sodium) and minimap mods are allowed. Any mod that gives an unfair advantage such as X-ray, Kill Aura or Auto-clicker is strictly forbidden and results in a permanent ban.
How often does the map reset?
Survival and SkyBlock maps reset at the start of each season, approximately every 3 months. Creative and Minigame maps do not reset. Season start dates are announced on Discord and the News page.
