General

  1. Respect all players and staff members at all times.
  2. No hate speech, discrimination or offensive language.
  3. No advertising other servers or websites in chat.
  4. Do not spam or flood the chat.
  5. English or Polish only in public chat.

Gameplay

  1. No hacking, cheating or using unfair mods.
  2. No griefing outside of designated PvP zones.
  3. No exploiting bugs or glitches – report them to staff.
  4. No stealing from other players' protected areas.
  5. No AFK farming or automated grinding without permission.

PvP

  1. PvP is only allowed in designated zones.
  2. No spawn killing or camping.
  3. No teaming in solo game modes.
  4. No using exploits to gain an unfair advantage.

Consequences

  1. Breaking rules may result in a warning, kick or ban.
  2. Severe violations result in a permanent ban without warning.
  3. Ban appeals can be submitted on our Discord server.
